# Annual reports

CCM publishes annual reports summarizing their work, impact, and funding each year. The PDFs are not tracked in this repo due to file size (~75MB total).

## Download them

All reports are available at: **[centerforcooperativemedia.org/annual-reports/](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/annual-reports/)**

## Available reports

| Year | Filename |
|------|----------|
| 2020 | `CCM-annual-report-2020-final.pdf` |
| 2021 | `2021 CCM Annual Report.pdf` |
| 2022 | `2022-CCM-annual-report.pdf` |
| 2023 | `2023 CCM annual report.pdf` |
| 2024 | `2024-CCM-annual-report-compressed.pdf` |

To work with these locally, download them from the link above and place them in this directory. The `.gitignore` excludes `reports/*.pdf`.
