# Spanish Translation News Service

**Type:** New Jersey initiative
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [centerforcooperativemedia.org/translation](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/translation/)

---

## Overview

The Spanish Translation News Service provides translation of local news content into Spanish, making reporting accessible to New Jersey's Spanish-speaking communities. The service was launched during the COVID-19 pandemic in response to the critical need for multilingual public health information.

## How it works

The service translates local news articles produced by NJ News Commons members into Spanish, expanding the reach of local journalism to communities that would otherwise be underserved by English-only outlets.
