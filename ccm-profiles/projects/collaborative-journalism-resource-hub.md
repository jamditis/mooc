## Overview

The Collaborative Journalism Resource Hub is the centerpiece of a $2.5 million Knight Foundation grant awarded in February 2025. It provides tools, coaching, financial support, and networking opportunities to support collaborative journalism projects across the United States.

The Hub continues work started under the Knight-funded Local Media Project, previously run by the Solutions Journalism Network.

## Goals

- Support **20 journalism collaboratives** across the U.S. over five years
- Launch **4 new collaboratives per year**
- Provide **up to $50,000** in financial support per collaborative
- Coaching focused on **revenue development and sustainability**
- Help collaboratives raise an additional **$2.5 million in local support** over five years
- By year two, at least **50% of collaboratives** should meet agreed-upon community impact metrics

## What the Hub provides

- Financial support (up to $50K per collaborative)
- Revenue development coaching
- Sustainability planning
- Impact tracking and measurement
- Networking with other collaboratives
- Governance and operations guidance
