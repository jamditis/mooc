# Civic Science Media Collaborations

**Type:** National initiative
**Scope:** National
**Funder:** Rita Allen Foundation (cross-field collaboration grants)

---

## Overview

The Civic Science Media Collaborations program bridges science communication and local journalism. It connects scientific institutions with local newsrooms to produce more accurate, community-relevant science reporting. The program awarded grants to 10 awardees and includes a civic science fellow (Catherine Devine).

This initiative reflects CCM's expansion into cross-field collaboration between journalism and other disciplines, working at the intersection of restoring community trust in both science and media.

## Key features

- Grants supporting interdisciplinary collaborative journalism projects
- 10 awardees in the current cohort
- Civic science fellow (Catherine Devine)
- Focus on breaking down silos between science and media
- Community trust building
