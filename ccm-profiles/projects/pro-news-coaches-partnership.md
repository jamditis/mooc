## Overview

A partnership with Pro News Coaches, a nonprofit staffed with former Wall Street Journal editors and reporters, to offer free coaching and editing services to eligible New Jersey newsrooms. The program provides professional-level editorial support to newsrooms that might not otherwise have access to experienced editors.

## Key features

- Pro bono coaching and editing
- Staffed by former Wall Street Journal editors and reporters
- Available to eligible NJ newsrooms
- Focus on improving editorial quality at small and local outlets
