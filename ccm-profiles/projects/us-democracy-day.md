**Contact:** will@usdemocracyday.org

## Overview

U.S. Democracy Day is a nationwide reporting collaborative involving 300+ newsrooms across the United States. Taking place annually on September 15, it promotes and centers pro-democracy journalism.

The project coordinates hundreds of participating newsrooms to produce journalism focused on democratic engagement, civic participation, and the state of democracy in communities across the country.

## History

Originally coordinated by Joe Amditis, the project has grown into one of the largest journalism collaborations in the United States. Will Fischer now serves as the dedicated project coordinator.
