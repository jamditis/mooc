# South Jersey Community Reporters

**Type:** NJ initiative / community journalism
**Scope:** South Jersey
**Partner:** NJ Council for the Humanities (NJCH)

---

## Overview

South Jersey Community Reporters is a joint initiative between the Center for Cooperative Media and the New Jersey Council for the Humanities. The program creates opportunities for residents of South Jersey to reshape how their communities produce, share, and engage with local news and information.

The initiative addresses a geographic gap in NJ's news ecosystem — much of the Center's work has historically been concentrated in North and Central Jersey, and this program extends support to the southern part of the state.

## Key features

- Community-driven reporting model
- Residents as active participants in news production
- Partnership between journalism and humanities organizations
- Focused on underserved South Jersey communities
