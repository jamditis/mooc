# NJ College News Commons

**Type:** NJ initiative / network
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [centerforcooperativemedia.org/njcollegenewscommons](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/njcollegenewscommons)

---

## Overview

The NJ College News Commons is a subset of the NJ News Commons specifically focused on connecting college journalism programs across New Jersey. It provides a network for student journalists and college media outlets to share resources, collaborate on reporting, and connect with professional journalists in the NJ News Commons network.

## Key features

- Network for college journalism programs in NJ
- Student journalist development
- Connection between academic and professional journalism
- Part of the broader NJ News Commons ecosystem
- Leverages Montclair State University's position as a leading communication and media school
