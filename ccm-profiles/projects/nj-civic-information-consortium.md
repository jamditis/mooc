# NJ Civic Information Consortium

**Type:** State body (hosted by CCM)
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [njcivicinfo.org](https://njcivicinfo.org/)

---

## Overview

The NJ Civic Information Consortium is a state-level body that the Center for Cooperative Media serves as host institution for. The Consortium supports civic information infrastructure in New Jersey, funding projects and initiatives that ensure residents have access to the news and information they need for civic participation.

Stefanie Murray serves as an advisor to the Consortium.

## Relationship to CCM

CCM is the host institution, providing administrative and operational support. The Consortium is a distinct entity with its own mission focused on civic information infrastructure, while CCM focuses specifically on local journalism and media collaboration.

## Key features

- State-funded civic information body
- Grants and funding for civic information projects
- Hosted at Montclair State University through CCM
- Focus on equitable access to civic information
