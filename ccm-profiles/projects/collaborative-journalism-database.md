## Overview

A searchable database tracking more than 1,000 journalism collaborations worldwide. The database is a core research resource maintained by the Center, allowing journalists, researchers, and newsroom leaders to explore models of collaboration across the globe.

The database is available in three languages:
- **English** — [collaborativejournalism.org/database](https://collaborativejournalism.org/database-search-sort-learn-collaborative-projects-around-world/)
- **Spanish** — [collaborativejournalism.org/database/spanish](https://collaborativejournalism.org/database-search-sort-learn-collaborative-projects-around-world/spanish/)
- **Portuguese** — [collaborativejournalism.org/database/portuguese](https://collaborativejournalism.org/database-search-sort-learn-collaborative-projects-around-world/portuguese/)

## Key features

- 1,000+ collaborations tracked
- Searchable and sortable
- Multilingual (English, Spanish, Portuguese)
- Open for submissions from the public
- Maintained by Mariela Santos-Muniz
