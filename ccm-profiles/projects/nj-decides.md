# NJ Decides 2025

**Type:** Collaborative election coverage
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [medium.com/centerforcooperativemedia (announcement)](https://medium.com/centerforcooperativemedia/nj-decides-2025-free-voter-guide-content-and-resources-for-new-jersey-newsrooms-63cb60f7358a)

---

## Overview

NJ Decides 2025 provides free, high-quality, republishable voter guide content and resources for New Jersey newsrooms covering the upcoming gubernatorial and legislative races. The initiative ensures that even small and under-resourced newsrooms can provide their audiences with election information.

## Key features

- Free voter guide content for NJ newsrooms
- Republishable across partner network
- Covers gubernatorial and legislative races
- Distributed through NJ News Commons partner network
