# GNI Sustainability Accelerator

**Type:** NJ initiative / services
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Partner:** Blue Engine Collaborative / Google News Initiative
**Website:** [blueenginecollaborative.com/state-accelerators](https://www.blueenginecollaborative.com/state-accelerators)

---

## Overview

The GNI Sustainability Accelerator provides dedicated coaching, expert audits, and peer collaboration to help local news organizations build stronger foundations for long-term success. It is run in partnership with Blue Engine Collaborative and supported by the Google News Initiative.

## Key features

- Dedicated sustainability coaching
- Expert audits of newsroom operations and revenue
- Peer collaboration among participating newsrooms
- Focus on long-term financial sustainability
- Part of a national network of state accelerators
