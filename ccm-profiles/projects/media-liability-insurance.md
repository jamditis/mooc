# Media Liability Insurance Options for NJ Newsrooms

**Type:** NJ initiative / services
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [medium.com/@etiennec (announcement)](https://medium.com/@etiennec/3ca21c558b29)

---

## Overview

A program providing affordable media liability insurance policies for small New Jersey newsrooms with less than $2 million in annual revenue. Many small and local newsrooms cannot afford standard media liability insurance, leaving them vulnerable to legal threats. This program negotiates group rates to make coverage accessible.

## Key features

- Targeted at newsrooms with under $2M annual revenue
- Affordable group rates
- Coaching on legal risk management
- Operational support for newsroom sustainability
