# Stories Invincible

**Type:** NJ initiative / community project
**Scope:** Camden, New Jersey
**Contact:** reetstarwind@gmail.com

---

## Overview

Stories Invincible is a community storytelling project focused on Camden, New Jersey. The project works to elevate restorative narratives about Camden's residents and history, countering deficit-based narratives that often dominate coverage of the city.

The project represents the Center's commitment to community-centered storytelling and amplifying voices from underserved areas of New Jersey.

## Key features

- Restorative narrative approach
- Community-centered storytelling
- Focus on Camden residents and history
- Art and media intersection
