# NJ Statewide Reporting Fellowship

**Type:** Fellowship program
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [centerforcooperativemedia.org/programs/fellowships/2025/statewide.html](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/programs/fellowships/2025/statewide.html)

---

## Overview

The NJ Statewide Reporting Fellowship is a multilingual reporting fellowship program that supports journalists covering critical issues across New Jersey. The current cohort of 15 reporters is covering topics including immigration, housing, education, and the gubernatorial race, bringing vital stories to their communities across the state.

## Key features

- **15 reporters** in the current cohort
- **Multilingual** reporting supported
- **Stipend** provided to fellows
- Covers statewide issues of public interest
- Content shared across NJ News Commons member network
