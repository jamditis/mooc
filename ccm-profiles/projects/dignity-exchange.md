# The Dignity Exchange

**Type:** National initiative
**Scope:** National
**Website:** [centerforcooperativemedia.org/dignity](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/dignity/)

---

## Overview

The Dignity Exchange is a national initiative creating a dignity-centered approach to news coverage. It encourages journalists and newsrooms to adopt practices that center human dignity in their reporting, particularly when covering marginalized or underserved communities.

## Key features

- Training on dignity-centered journalism practices
- Collaboration among newsrooms adopting the approach
- Network-building across participating organizations
