# 2025 Investigative Reporting Fellowships

**Type:** Fellowship / training program
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [medium.com/centerforcooperativemedia (announcement)](https://medium.com/centerforcooperativemedia/center-for-cooperative-media-launches-2025-investigative-reporting-initiative-6dbe5f0eec5b)

---

## Overview

A statewide investigative journalism training program for New Jersey-based newsrooms. The program provides reporting support, training, and resources to strengthen investigative journalism capacity across the state.

## Key features

- Targeted at NJ-based newsrooms
- Training in investigative reporting techniques
- Reporting support and resources provided
- Part of CCM's broader effort to strengthen local journalism capacity
