# Excellence in Community Media Awards + Celebration

**Type:** Annual event
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [centerforcooperativemedia.org/ecmexpo](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/ecmexpo)

---

## Overview

An annual event celebrating the diverse voices shaping New Jersey's media landscape. The awards recognize outstanding work by NJ News Commons members and the broader NJ journalism community. Previously known as the Excellence in Local News Awards, the event includes monetary prizes for winners.

The celebration is paired with the NJ Ethnic and Community Media Expo, which highlights the contributions of ethnic and community media organizations across the state.

## Key features

- Annual awards ceremony
- Monetary prizes for winners
- Celebration of NJ's diverse media ecosystem
- Paired with Ethnic and Community Media Expo
