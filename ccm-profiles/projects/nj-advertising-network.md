# New Jersey Advertising Network (Ad Lab)

**Type:** NJ initiative / collaboration
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Website:** [centerforcooperativemedia.org/adlab](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/adlab/)

---

## Overview

The New Jersey Advertising Network (also known as the Ad Lab) enables New Jersey newsrooms to collaborate on advertising strategies to enhance revenue sustainability. By pooling resources and working together on ad sales, small and local newsrooms can offer advertisers broader reach while generating more revenue individually.

## Key features

- Collective advertising strategies for NJ newsrooms
- Revenue sustainability focus
- Collaboration among member newsrooms
- Part of the NJ News Commons ecosystem
