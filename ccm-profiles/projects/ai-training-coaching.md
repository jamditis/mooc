# AI Training and Coaching

**Type:** National initiative / support program
**Scope:** National
**Website:** [centerforcooperativemedia.org/resources/ai](https://centerforcooperativemedia.org/resources/ai/)

---

## Overview

Training and coaching programs to help journalists responsibly integrate AI tools into their reporting and newsroom operations. The Center has published several guides on generative AI for small and local newsrooms, authored primarily by Joe Amditis.

## Key features

- Training sessions on AI tools for journalism
- One-on-one coaching for newsrooms
- Published guides and educational resources on generative AI
- Focus on responsible and ethical AI integration
- Available to NJ News Commons members and the broader journalism community

## Staff

- **Joe Amditis** — Author of AI guides and resources; leads operational aspects
