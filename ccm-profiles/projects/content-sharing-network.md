# Statewide Content-sharing Network

**Type:** NJ initiative / collaboration
**Scope:** New Jersey (statewide)
**Partner:** NJ Spotlight News
**Website:** [medium.com/centerforcooperativemedia (announcement)](https://medium.com/centerforcooperativemedia/nj-news-commons-nj-spotlight-news-to-launch-content-sharing-and-advertising-network-a0e4a652c31f)

---

## Overview

A statewide content-sharing and advertising network developed in partnership with NJ Spotlight News. The network enables NJ News Commons members to share and republish content, expanding the reach of local journalism across the state. It also includes an advertising component to help member newsrooms generate revenue collectively.

## Key features

- Content sharing among NJ News Commons member newsrooms
- Advertising network for collective revenue generation
- Partnership with NJ Spotlight News
- Launched late 2025
- Built on earlier content-sharing efforts that began during Hurricane Sandy and expanded during the COVID-19 pandemic
