## Background

Delaney Butler previously served as the program operations manager at Solutions Journalism Network, where she connected and coordinated operational processes for SJN's programmatic work. She joined SJN in 2018 as an operations associate before joining the dedicated team of the Knight-supported Local Media Project, where she led operational start-up and scaffolding before overseeing collaborative projects and facilitating the initiative's cohort of project managers.

Before SJN, Delaney worked at Prudential Financial's Office of Corporate Social Responsibility, holding coordination and support roles in the Spirit of Community Awards program (now the Prudential Emerging Visionaries program) and the Prudential Foundation.

Born and raised in Morris County, she now lives in Manhattan.

## Education

- **B.A.** — English and American Literature, New York University, College of Arts and Sciences

## Role at CCM

Delaney serves as assistant director of the Collaborative Journalism Resource Hub. She oversees new collaborative projects, connects with and coaches journalists and organizations interested in collaboration, and maintains a specialization in operations and governance. Her transition from Solutions Journalism Network provides institutional knowledge and continuity to the Hub's work.
