## Background

Noelle Swan is a veteran journalist with a background in science reporting, special project management, and collaborative facilitation. She spent 12 years at The Christian Science Monitor, where she helmed the science, environment, and technology desk before becoming Editor of the Weekly magazine.

## Role at CCM

Noelle manages the Center's civic science media collaborations program, which bridges science communication and local journalism. The program awarded grants to 10 awardees and includes a civic science fellow (Catherine Devine). Her work sits at the intersection of restoring community trust in both science and media — connecting scientific institutions with local newsrooms for more accurate, community-relevant science reporting.

This is a newer position at the Center, reflecting CCM's expansion into cross-field collaboration between journalism and other disciplines.
