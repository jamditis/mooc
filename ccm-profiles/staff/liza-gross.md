## Background

Liza Gross is a journalist and media leader with over three decades of experience working in executive positions at news organizations and nonprofits. She has specialized in the transition of traditional news outlets to multimedia platforms and in the exploration of new models for media organizations, including collaborative arrangements and audience engagement techniques.

Key positions held:
- **Managing Editor** — The Miami Herald
- **Executive Editor** — El Nuevo Dia (Puerto Rico)
- **Publisher** — Exito! (Spanish language publication of the Chicago Tribune)
- **Executive Director and board member** — International Women's Media Foundation (supported female journalists in conflict zones and created professional opportunities for women in media)
- **Board member** — National Association of Hispanic Journalists

She has conducted seminars and training for journalists in the United States, Latin America, and Europe.

## Role at CCM

Liza serves as senior advisor to the Collaborative Journalism Resource Hub, providing continuity from the previous Knight-funded Local Media Project at Solutions Journalism Network, which she spearheaded. Her experience in both English and Spanish-language media and her background in collaborative and innovative media models make her a key strategic voice for the Hub.
