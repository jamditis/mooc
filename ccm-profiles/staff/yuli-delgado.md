## Background

Yuliana Delgado is the assistant coordinator for the Spanish Translation News Service. She has more than 20 years of experience in the media industry, both in print and digital.

She began her career at Latina Magazine in New York City. Moving into digital, Yuli was one of the founding editors of MamásLatinas, the country's only website dedicated to the bilingual, bicultural mom. She has also worked on independent projects translating, proofreading, and editing Spanish language content for organizations like Cancer and Careers, Viacom, and the Pan American Health Organization.

## Education

- **B.A.** — English and Anthropology, Barnard College, Columbia University

## Role at CCM

Yuli manages the Spanish Translation News Service, making local news content accessible to New Jersey's Spanish-speaking communities. Her two decades of experience in bilingual media and her work with organizations like the Pan American Health Organization make her a strong fit for bridging the language gap in NJ's news ecosystem.
