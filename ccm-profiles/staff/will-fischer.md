## Background

Will Fischer previously worked for Business Insider as a Senior Editor and Strategist on the Service Journalism team. He's also been a Fact-Checker at New York Magazine.

At Northwestern's Medill School of Journalism, he conducted research on local news ecosystems and helped City Bureau launch their Documenters program by attending town halls and doing interviews with community stakeholders.

He lives in New York with his wife and two cats.

## Education

- **B.A./M.S.** — Medill School of Journalism, Northwestern University

## Role at CCM

Will coordinates U.S. Democracy Day, a nationwide reporting collaborative involving 300+ newsrooms that takes place annually on September 15. Democracy Day promotes and centers pro-democracy journalism across the United States. The project requires coordinating hundreds of participating newsrooms, managing shared resources, and ensuring a unified national effort around democratic engagement coverage.
