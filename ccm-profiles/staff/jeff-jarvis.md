# Jeff Jarvis

**Title:** Distinguished Senior Fellow

---

## Background

Jeff Jarvis is a prominent media commentator, author, and journalism innovator. Until 2005, he was president and creative director of Advance.net, the online arm of Advance Publications.

Career highlights:
- **Creator and founding editor** — Entertainment Weekly
- **Sunday editor and associate publisher** — New York Daily News
- **TV critic** — TV Guide and People
- **Columnist** — San Francisco Examiner
- **Assistant city editor and reporter** — Chicago Tribune
- **Blogger** — Buzzmachine.com (media and news commentary)
- **Podcast co-host** — This Week in Google

## Books

- *Gutenberg the Geek* (Kindle Single)
- *What Would Google Do?* (HarperCollins, 2009)
- *Public Parts: How Sharing in the Digital Age Improves the Way We Work and Live* (Simon & Schuster, 2011)
- *Geeks Bearing Gifts: Imagining New Futures for News* (CUNY Journalism Press, 2014)

## Role at CCM

As Distinguished Senior Fellow, Jeff Jarvis brings decades of media industry leadership and thought leadership to the Center. His work at the intersection of technology and journalism, combined with his extensive writing on the future of news, supports the Center's mission to innovate in local journalism.
