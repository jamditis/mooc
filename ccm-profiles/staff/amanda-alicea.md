## Background

Amanda Alicea is a freelancing feature reporter and writer who serves as the data entry and membership coordinator at the Center. As a New Jersey native, she covers places, people, and events in her community. She was a feature reporter for Montclair State's student-run newspaper, The Montclarion, and worked closely with the radio station as a newscaster on 90.3 FM WMSC.

She currently also works as a news producer for KUSA (9NEWS) in Colorado.

## Education

- **B.A.** — Journalism, School of Communication and Media, Montclair State University (2022)

## Role at CCM

Amanda manages data entry and membership coordination for the NJ News Commons, the Center's flagship network of 300+ news and information providers. She tracks member organizations, processes new applications, and maintains the membership database.
