## Background

Anthony "Oni" Advincula is a New York City-based journalist and communications consultant. He formerly served as the national media director, writer, and editor for New America Media, where he managed and organized ethnic media projects in 45 states.

He was a correspondent for The Jersey Journal and the communications director and managing editor of the Independent Press Association-New York, where he co-edited Voices That Must Be Heard (now known as CUNY's Voices of NY).

## Education

- **Dual M.A.** — Public administration and journalism, Columbia University (Charles H. Revson Fellowship)
- Advanced narrative nonfiction studies at Harvard University

## Awards and fellowships

- New York Times Foreign Press Fellowship
- National Health Journalism Fellowship
- Charles H. Revson Fellowship at Columbia University

## Role at CCM

Oni coordinates the Center's ethnic and community media outreach across New Jersey, connecting with diverse media outlets and supporting multilingual journalism. His national experience managing ethnic media projects in 45 states gives him a unique perspective on building inclusive media networks at the state level.
