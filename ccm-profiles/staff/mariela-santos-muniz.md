## Background

Mariela Santos-Muniz is a freelance journalist and writer based in Puerto Rico. Her reporting focuses on Latinx issues and has appeared in Nylon, The Daily Dot, Bustle, The Open Notebook, Ensia, and other outlets.

## Education

- **M.A.** — International Relations and International Communications, Boston University
- **B.A.** — Humanities, Universidad del Turabo (now Universidad Ana G. Mendez, Gurabo)

## Role at CCM

Mariela updates the collaborative journalism database and curates the biweekly collaborative journalism newsletter, which covers partnership and collaboration developments in journalism across the U.S. and globally. She contributes to maintaining the Center's database of 1,000+ journalism collaborations worldwide, which is available in English, Spanish, and Portuguese at collaborativejournalism.org.
