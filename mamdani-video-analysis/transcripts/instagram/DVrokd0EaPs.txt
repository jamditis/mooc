Spring is almost here, and something beautiful is ready to bloom across New York City. The expansion of 3K. This fall, we're adding more than 1,000 seats to 3K programs in 56 different zip codes across the five boroughs. That's more than half of all school districts in New York City. This will help families that want to enroll their kid in 3K find a seat that actually works for them. In the past, families would eagerly apply for 3K, only to find their child has been placed miles away from their home, leaving them to either shell out for a private provider or leave the city altogether. A three-year-old can't take themselves to class three miles away, and a working parent shouldn't have to commute from Parkchester to Park Slope just to find childcare. That's why our administration is working tirelessly to make sure that every family can get the more than $20,000 in savings a year that 3K provides them. And so every child can receive the benefits of an early childhood education. And as we continue to expand 3K, we're also working to ensure that students with disabilities have greater access to services and program options, especially in communities with unmet needs. If you've already applied for 3K, you don't have to do anything. NYC schools will notify you as seats are added in your community. You can also update your application up until April 24th to include new programs. Our administration believes that every child deserves to have the chance to blossom. And what you water grows.
