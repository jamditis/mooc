New York City, it is the leading city in the entire world, not in the height of its building, but in the services to its people. It is a privilege to serve as the chief magistrate of a community of eight million people. It was the spirit of Theodore Roosevelt and Franklin Roosevelt, and they dared to believe that public interest must prevail over special interest. I do not exaggerate when I say that New York is unique in the history of humankind. We have come a long way, so we gather here in the fresh chill of a new year to begin a new administration. Dreaming demands hope. Dreaming demands solidarity. Dreaming demands work. If this campaign has demonstrated anything, it is that our dreams can become reality. This is not my victory. This is ours. Thank you.
